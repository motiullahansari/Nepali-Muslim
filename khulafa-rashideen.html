The Four Rightly-Guided Caliphs

The blessed companions who carried the Khilafat after the Prophet ﷺ — each a pillar of Islam.

١
أَبُو بَكْرٍ الصِّدِّيق
Abu Bakr al-Siddiq RA
🌟 Al-Siddiq — The Truthful
📅 573 – 634 CE ⏳ Khilafat: 11–13 AH 🕐 2 Years, 3 Months

The closest and most beloved companion of the Prophet ﷺ. First adult male to accept Islam. Accompanied the Prophet ﷺ in the Hijrah. Became the first Caliph after the Prophet's ﷺ passing.

  • First to believe in Isra wal Mi'raj without hesitation
  • Crushed the apostasy (Riddah) wars with firm resolve
  • Ordered compilation of the Quran into one Mushaf
  • Launched conquests of Persia and Byzantine Syria
٢
عُمَرُ بْنُ الخَطَّاب
Umar ibn al-Khattab RA
⚖️ Al-Farooq — Distinguisher of Truth
📅 584 – 644 CE ⏳ Khilafat: 13–23 AH 🕐 10 Years, 6 Months

One of the greatest leaders in Islamic history. His acceptance of Islam was a turning point for the early Muslims. Renowned for justice, simplicity, and expanding the Islamic state across two superpowers.

  • Conquered Persia, Syria, Egypt, and Iraq
  • Introduced the Hijri calendar
  • Established Bayt al-Mal (public treasury)
  • Martyred by Abu Lu'lu'ah al-Majusi in 23 AH
٣
عُثْمَانُ بْنُ عَفَّان
Uthman ibn Affan RA
🔑 Dhul-Nurayn — Possessor of Two Lights
📅 576 – 656 CE ⏳ Khilafat: 23–35 AH 🕐 12 Years

Known for his extraordinary generosity and modesty. Married two daughters of the Prophet ﷺ. His greatest legacy is the standardization of the Quran — the Uthmani Mushaf still used today.

  • Compiled and standardized the Uthmani Mushaf
  • Married Ruqayyah RA, then Umm Kulthum RA — both daughters of the Prophet ﷺ
  • Expanded the Prophet's ﷺ Masjid in Madinah
  • Martyred while reciting the Quran in 35 AH
٤
عَلِيُّ بْنُ أَبِي طَالِب
Ali ibn Abi Talib RA
🦁 Asadullah — Lion of Allah
📅 601 – 661 CE ⏳ Khilafat: 35–40 AH 🕐 4 Years, 9 Months

Cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet ﷺ, husband of Fatimah RA. Among the first to accept Islam. Renowned for his bravery, knowledge, and eloquence. The Prophet ﷺ said: "I am the city of knowledge and Ali is its gate."

  • First child to accept Islam
  • Slept in the Prophet's ﷺ bed on the night of Hijrah
  • Husband of Fatimah RA, father of Hasan & Husayn RA
  • Martyred by Ibn Muljam in Kufa, 40 AH
