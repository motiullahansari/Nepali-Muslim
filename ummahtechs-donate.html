FAQs

Common Questions

Why is there no online payment gateway yet?

We're currently in the process of setting up secure international payment options. In the meantime, we use Email and WhatsApp for direct, personalized donations — this also builds trust with donors.

Can I donate from anywhere in the world?

Yes! We accept donations from anywhere in the world. Contact us via email or WhatsApp and we'll arrange the best method based on your location and currency.

Is my donation Zakat eligible?

Currently, UmmahTechs is not a registered Zakat recipient. Your donations are considered Sadaqah Jariyah (continuous charity) — earning rewards as long as our resources benefit the Ummah, inshaAllah.

Can I donate anonymously?

Absolutely. You can request anonymity when you contact us, and your identity will be kept completely confidential.

Can my organization sponsor a specific project?

Yes! Organizations and individuals can sponsor specific projects — like a translation, a book, or a section. Contact us to discuss details.
