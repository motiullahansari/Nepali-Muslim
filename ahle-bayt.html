Ahle Bayt — The Blessed Household

إِنَّمَا يُرِيدُ اللَّهُ لِيُذْهِبَ عَنكُمُ الرِّجْسَ أَهْلَ الْبَيْتِ وَيُطَهِّرَكُمْ تَطْهِيرًا

"Allah only wishes to remove evil from you, O People of the Household, and to purify you with a thorough purification."

Surah Al-Ahzab 33:33
إِنِّي تَارِكٌ فِيكُمُ الثَّقَلَيْنِ، كِتَابَ اللَّهِ وَعِتْرَتِي أَهْلَ بَيْتِي

"I am leaving among you two weighty things — the Book of Allah and my family, the People of my Household."

— Sahih Muslim 2408
