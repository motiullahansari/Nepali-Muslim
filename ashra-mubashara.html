⭐ Ashara Mubashshara

The Ten Promised Paradise

These ten Companions were explicitly named by the Prophet ﷺ in a single hadith as people of Jannah.

عَنْ عَبْدِ الرَّحْمَنِ بْنِ عَوْفٍ قَالَ قَالَ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ ﷺ: أَبُو بَكْرٍ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَعُمَرُ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَعُثْمَانُ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَعَلِيٌّ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَطَلْحَةُ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَالزُّبَيْرُ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَعَبْدُ الرَّحْمَنِ بْنُ عَوْفٍ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَسَعْدٌ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَسَعِيدٌ فِي الجَنَّةِ، وَأَبُو عُبَيْدَةَ بْنُ الجَرَّاحِ فِي الجَنَّةِ

Abd al-Rahman ibn Awf narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said: "Abu Bakr is in Paradise, Umar is in Paradise, Uthman is in Paradise, Ali is in Paradise, Talha is in Paradise, al-Zubayr is in Paradise, Abd al-Rahman ibn Awf is in Paradise, Sa'd is in Paradise, Sa'id is in Paradise, and Abu Ubaydah ibn al-Jarrah is in Paradise."

Tirmidhi 3747 · Abu Dawud 4649 · Ibn Majah 133 — Sahih
👑 Also Khulafa-e-Rashideen
١
أَبُو بَكْرٍ الصِّدِّيق
Abu Bakr al-Siddiq RA
🌟 Al-Siddiq — The Truthful
📅 573 – 634 CE 📍 Makkah 1st Caliph

Closest companion and father-in-law of the Prophet ﷺ. First adult male to accept Islam. Accompanied the Prophet ﷺ in the Hijrah. Led the Ummah as the first Caliph after the Prophet's ﷺ passing.

  • First to believe in Isra wal Mi'raj without hesitation
  • Crushed Riddah wars; ordered compilation of the Quran
٢
عُمَرُ بْنُ الخَطَّاب
Umar ibn al-Khattab RA
⚖️ Al-Farooq — Distinguisher of Truth
📅 584 – 644 CE 📍 Makkah 2nd Caliph

Renowned for justice, strength, and simplicity. His acceptance of Islam was a turning point for the early Muslims. Expanded the Islamic state across Persia and Byzantium in just 10 years.

  • Conquered Persia, Syria, Egypt, and Iraq
  • Introduced the Hijri calendar; established Bayt al-Mal
٣
عُثْمَانُ بْنُ عَفَّان
Uthman ibn Affan RA
🔑 Dhul-Nurayn — Possessor of Two Lights
📅 576 – 656 CE 📍 Makkah 3rd Caliph

Known for extraordinary generosity and modesty. Married two daughters of the Prophet ﷺ. His greatest legacy — the Uthmani Mushaf — is the standardized Quran still used by every Muslim today.

  • Standardized the Uthmani Mushaf of the Quran
  • Martyred while reciting the Quran, 35 AH
٤
عَلِيُّ بْنُ أَبِي طَالِب
Ali ibn Abi Talib RA
🦁 Asadullah — Lion of Allah
📅 601 – 661 CE 📍 Makkah 4th Caliph

Cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet ﷺ. First child to accept Islam. Renowned for bravery, knowledge, and eloquence. Husband of Fatimah RA, father of Hasan and Husayn RA.

  • Slept in Prophet's ﷺ bed on the night of Hijrah
  • Martyred by Ibn Muljam in Kufa, 40 AH
⭐ Remaining Six
٥
طَلْحَةُ بْنُ عُبَيْدِ اللَّه
Talha ibn Ubaydullah RA
🛡️ Talhat al-Khayr — Talha the Good
📅 594 – 656 CE 📍 Makkah Tribe: Taym

Among the earliest converts to Islam. At Uhud he shielded the Prophet ﷺ with his own body, losing the use of his hand permanently. Renowned across Arabia for his extraordinary generosity.

  • Shielded the Prophet ﷺ at Uhud with his body
  • Called "Talha the Generous" for his immense charity
٦
الزُّبَيْرُ بْنُ العَوَّام
Zubayr ibn al-Awwam RA
⚔️ Hawari al-Rasul — Disciple of the Prophet ﷺ
📅 594 – 656 CE 📍 Makkah Nephew of Khadijah RA

Cousin of the Prophet ﷺ and one of the earliest Muslims. The Prophet ﷺ called him his "disciple." A fierce warrior who participated in nearly every major battle of early Islam.

  • First to draw his sword in the path of Islam
  • Son of Safiyyah bint Abd al-Muttalib, aunt of the Prophet ﷺ
٧
عَبْدُ الرَّحْمَنِ بْنُ عَوْف
Abd al-Rahman ibn Awf RA
💰 Al-Jawwad — The Most Generous
📅 580 – 652 CE 📍 Makkah Tribe: Zuhrah

One of the wealthiest Companions, yet among the most generous. Migrated to Madinah with nothing and rebuilt his fortune quickly. Donated enormous wealth — horses, camels, and gold — in the way of Allah.

  • Donated 500 horses and 1,500 camels for jihad
  • Led Salah when the Prophet ﷺ was absent
٨
سَعْدُ بْنُ أَبِي وَقَّاص
Sa'd ibn Abi Waqqas RA
🏹 First Archer in Islam
📅 595 – 674 CE 📍 Makkah Maternal uncle of Prophet ﷺ

The first person to shoot an arrow in the path of Allah. The Prophet ﷺ honored him by saying "May my father and mother be your ransom" — a distinction given to very few companions.

  • First archer in the path of Allah
  • Conquered Persia at the Battle of al-Qadisiyyah
٩
سَعِيدُ بْنُ زَيْد
Sa'id ibn Zayd RA
🌿 Min al-Sabiqeen — Among the Forerunners
📅 ~593 – 671 CE 📍 Makkah Brother-in-law of Umar RA

One of the earliest Muslims, accepting Islam even before the Prophet ﷺ entered Dar al-Arqam. Brother-in-law of Umar RA. Participated in almost all major battles alongside the Prophet ﷺ.

  • Accepted Islam among the very first
  • Participated in all major battles of Islam
١٠
أَبُو عُبَيْدَةَ بْنُ الجَرَّاح
Abu Ubaydah ibn al-Jarrah RA
🤝 Ameen al-Ummah — Trustee of the Ummah
📅 583 – 639 CE 📍 Makkah Tribe: Fahr

The Prophet ﷺ called him "the trustee of this Ummah." Known for remarkable humility, trustworthiness, and selflessness. Commanded the Muslim armies in Syria. Died in the Amwas plague as a martyr.

  • Called "Ameen al-Ummah" by the Prophet ﷺ
  • Led conquest of Syria; died in Amwas plague, 18 AH
رَضِيَ اللَّهُ عَنْهُمْ وَرَضُوا عَنْهُ

"Allah is pleased with them and they are pleased with Him."

— Surah Al-Bayyinah 98:8
