Our Story

How It All Started

UmmahTechs was born from a critical gap. In India and Nepal, millions of Muslims lacked access to authentic Islamic knowledge in their own languages — Hindi and Nepali. The available resources were fragmented, unverified, or filled with misinformation.

The project was established to protect and strengthen faith in a region where digital propaganda and misinformation are widespread. We aimed to build a single, trusted ecosystem covering Seerat, Quran, Hadees, and Islamic history.

With independent data control, multilingual support, and a long-term vision, UmmahTechs is the first serious platform designed specifically for minority Muslims in South Asia — combining accuracy, accessibility, and modern technology.

Today, we serve a growing community of learners worldwide — and we're just getting started. InshaAllah, with your support, we'll continue building until every Muslim has authentic Islamic knowledge at their fingertips.
